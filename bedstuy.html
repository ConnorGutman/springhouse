Bed-Stuy

Higher Education

With a “built-in” market of new apartment seekers and relative insulation from neighborhood economic swings, student housing can be a very lucrative investment. That’s why Springhouse identified this Bedford Avenue property in Clinton Hill, Brooklyn as an great opportunity. It boasts 43 units - both furnished and unfurnished - solid architecture, and, most importantly - convenient walking and public transportation access to Parsons, The New School, The Culinary Institute, NYU, and other institutions.

64,000 square feet

43 furnished and unfurnished apartments

9-story elevator building

With convenient access to Pratt, Parsons, The New School, and New York University, this 9 story, 64,000 square feet building presents a tremendous opportunity for singles, recent grads, and young families.

A staggered, set-back profile and brick construction details integrate with the neighborhood architecture

Constructed in 2004, the asset features 43 unit, many with balconies and terraces
