Williamsburg

The New Creative Class

With this property, Springhouse saw an opportunity to tap into the growth and increasing affluence of East Williamsburg and its younger, “creative” class audiences. The property features a prime location in a vibrant neighborhood, close proximity to public transit, and a range of on-site amenities such as a gym, game room, and a massive rooftop deck with skyline views of Manhattan. Moreover, many of the units can easy be converted into shared apartments, making them especially attractive to the young tenants.

32 loft-style apartments

4-story building
