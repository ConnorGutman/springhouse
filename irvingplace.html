Irving Place

Untapped Value at Gramercy Park

Successful investments begin by identifying potential value where others do not. The property at Irving Place and 17th St. is a prime example. Here, Springhouse saw a highly advantaged Gramercy Park location and a severely mismanaged property, resulting in below market rents. By reconfiguring and overhauling the space and enhancing the operations, Springhouse was able to attract a new group of tenants - including retail, commercial, and individuals - willing to pay a premium for a prestige location.
